Service Designer and User Researcher

Welcome! I am a Service Designer and User Researcher specializing in creating seamless and user-centric experiences. I work at the intersection of design, technology, and user behavior to shape innovative solutions that meet user needs and business goals.

As a Service Designer, I analyze and improve end-to-end customer journeys, focusing on the holistic experience and identifying opportunities for enhancement. I conduct thorough user research to understand user behaviors, pain points, and aspirations.

My services include:

  • Design Thinking Workshops: Collaborative sessions to identify opportunities, brainstorm ideas, and define project goals.
  • User Research: In-depth qualitative and quantitative research methods to uncover user insights and validate design decisions.
  • Service Blueprinting: Mapping out the entire service ecosystem to visualize touchpoints, interactions, and pain points.
  • Prototyping and Testing: Creating interactive prototypes and conducting usability testing to refine user experiences.
  • UX/UI Design: Crafting intuitive and visually appealing interfaces that align with user needs and business objectives.

If you are looking to enhance your services, improve user experiences, or collaborate on exciting projects, I would love to hear from you. Please don't hesitate to get in touch to discuss how we can work together.
